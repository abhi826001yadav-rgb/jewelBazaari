One product record appears across all matching sections automatically.

Photo rules

Upload up to 3 photos per jewellery item. Only JPG, JPEG, and PNG formats are accepted.

Every photo is automatically cropped to a 1:1 square (1200 × 1200 px) and compressed to max 250 KB for fast loading, while keeping good quality.

After a successful upload, your photos are saved in the jewelBazaari database and your product goes live on the website.

Use bright, clear photos on a plain background for the best storefront display.

Example: 14kt Rose Gold ring with Diamond → Metal Type “14kt rose gold”, Stone “Diamond”, Category “Rings”.
