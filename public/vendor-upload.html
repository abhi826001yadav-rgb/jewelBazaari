Photo rules

Upload up to 5 photos per jewellery item. Images are saved securely in Cloudinary.

We auto-crop to a 4:5 portrait ratio, resize to 1200 × 1500 px, and compress each photo to 1 MB or less in WebP format.

Use bright, clear photos on a plain background for the best storefront display.

Example: Diamond + Gold Ring → appears in All Jewellery, Diamond, Gold, and Rings.
